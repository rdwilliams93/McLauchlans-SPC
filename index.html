About Us

After spending last summer in Mexico, my girlfriend and I came across a Mexican man just making normal fresh crisps on the street; the theatre of it was fantastic. You got to see the potato go from raw state to finished product in a matter of minutes! These crisps were incredible. Hot, crunchy and full of flavour, heaps better than your packaged product. That's when I had this light bulb moment. Why can't this be recreated in the UK with an alternative root vegetable, such as the sweet potato?

The consumer is becoming more aware of what they are eating, and what we do at McLauchlan’s is break down all the barriers and show the consumer exactly what goes in to their snack.

Come find me and my trusty cargo bike at some of the best markets the city has to offer! Cooking up the freshest, best tasting vegetable crisps around.

We are available for private hire at corporate events, parties or anything you can throw at us! We love a party and have worked with clients including MTV and The Royal Warrants Association.
