About Us

After spending last summer in Mexico, my girlfriend and I came across a Mexican man just making normal fresh crisps on the street; the theatre of it was fantastic. You got to see the potato go from raw state to finished product in a matter of minutes! These crisps were incredible. Hot, crunchy and full of flavour, heaps better than your packaged product. That's when I had this light bulb moment. Why can't this be recreated in the UK with an alternative root vegetable, such as the sweet potato?
